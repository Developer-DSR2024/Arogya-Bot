# 📱 ArogyaBot — Main App

## ▶️ How to Open
1. Open **ArogyaBot_Final.html** in **Google Chrome**
2. Everything works instantly — no setup needed!

---

## 🎯 How to Use Each Feature

### 💬 AI Chat
- Type symptoms in the input box → press Send or Enter
- Or click quick chips: "Fever & headache", "Stomach pain", etc.
- AI replies with diagnosis, home remedies & severity

### 🎤 Voice Input
- Click the 🎤 mic button
- Speak your symptoms in Hindi or English
- AI will automatically process what you said

### 🌐 Language Toggle
- Click **EN** or **हि** button in top right corner
- Entire app switches language instantly
- AI also replies in Hindi when Hindi is selected

### 🏥 Hospital Finder
- Click **Hospitals** tab
- Click **"Find Nearby Hospitals"**
- Allow location permission when browser asks
- Shows map + list of nearby hospitals with distance
- Click **"Directions"** to open Google Maps navigation

### 🔔 Medicine Reminders
- Click **Reminders** tab
- Fill in: Medicine name, Dosage, Frequency, Time, Duration
- Click **"Add Reminder"**
- Click **WhatsApp** button to share reminder with family
- Click 🗑️ to delete a reminder

---

## ☁️ Database Integration
- Every AI chat is **automatically saved** to Supabase cloud
- Every reminder is **synced to cloud** — works across devices
- Open Supabase dashboard to show judges live data!

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes
- Must use **Google Chrome** (voice input only works in Chrome)
- Allow **location permission** for hospital finder
- Needs **internet connection** for AI chat & hospital finder
